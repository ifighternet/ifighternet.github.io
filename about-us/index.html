About Us

iFighter was founded in 2011 by Liam McNamee, a 3rd-degree black belt in Tae Kwon Do with over 18 years of martial arts experience.

iFighter Tae Kwon Do started as just one club but has grown to 3 clubs, Keady, Ravensdale and Jonesborough.
